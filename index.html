About

About System

Focused on phishing detection in modern cybersecurity environments, with emphasis on identifying malicious URLs, deceptive email content, and spoofed domains. The approach combines machine learning, threat intelligence, and behavioral indicators to detect attacks early and reduce false positives.

Core capabilities include URL reputation checks, suspicious keyword and structure analysis, and email-content risk scoring. The system is designed to give clear, fast, and understandable threat signals for both technical and non-technical users.

Functional advantages include early warning for phishing campaigns, improved decision-making before clicking unknown links, reduced false positives through layered checks, and better overall cyber awareness during daily browsing and email usage.

This approach supports safer workflows by helping users identify spoofed domains, detect social-engineering patterns, and act quickly on high-risk indicators before damage occurs.
